Help Center

Everything you need to know to use Sky Resume smoothly.

Getting Started

How do I start building a resume?

Click on the "Start Resume" button on the homepage. You don’t need an account—just pick a template and start typing.

Do I need to download software?

No! Sky Resume is fully online and works in your browser on any device.

Premium Features

What is included in Premium?

Premium unlock gives you all 30+ templates, high-quality PDF downloads, custom colors, and removes the watermark.

How do I pay for Premium?

You can pay via Paystack or Flutterwave. Once payment is confirmed, premium features are unlocked instantly for life.

Troubleshooting

My PDF download isn’t working. What should I do?

Check your internet connection, refresh the page, and try again. If it still fails, contact us via Sky Resume Buzz.

Who can I contact for technical support?

Use our community page Sky Resume Buzz or submit feedback through SkyWeb Developers.
